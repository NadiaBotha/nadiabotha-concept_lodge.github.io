About Concept Lodge

Concept Lodge is the ideal location for anyone looking to enjoy the great outdoors. We have a variety of accomodation options, ranging from camping lots to luxurious five-star hotel rooms. Whether you want to cook your own dinner on an open fire, or order room-service, Concept Lodge is the place for you.

Located at the foothills of the Maluti Moutain Range and only 3.3km from Clarens, there are plenty of outdoor and other tourist activities to do during the day. With various swimming locations, game drives and hikes, it truely is the vacation destination for the whole family, with a child-friendly and relaxing atmopshere.
